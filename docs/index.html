About

This page hosts the TON Connect manifest for @tonrafflebest_bot. The manifest allows TON wallets to securely connect and interact with our raffle bot.

Features

  • Connect your TON wallet once
  • One-click payments for raffle entries
  • Secure transaction signing
  • Support for all major TON wallets
